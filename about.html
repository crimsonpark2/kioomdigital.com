About Kioom Digital

We are the parent company behind a growing portfolio of digital platforms, each solving unique problems with bold design and solid engineering.

Our Story

🎯 Why We Exist

Kioom Digital was founded with a simple belief: every underserved market deserves a purpose-built digital platform. Instead of building one product for everyone, we build many products for specific people.

We are the mother company — the orchestrator, the funder, the strategist — behind platforms like Lifetone.life and TradieHQ. Our team doesn't just launch apps; we launch businesses.

🏢 Our Structure

We operate as a Sole Trader with registered ABN, allowing us to move fast while maintaining full control. This lean structure means decisions happen quickly, profits aren't diluted, and every project gets founder-level attention.

ABN (Australian Business Number): Available upon request for verification.

📍 Our Base

Headquartered in Australia, we serve clients and users globally. Our team is distributed, remote-friendly, and focused on outcomes, not office hours.

Mission & Vision

🚀 Mission

🔭 Vision
